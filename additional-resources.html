IRIS

The IRIS program provides adults with an option to aid their long-term care needs. Those who are enrolled manage their own goods and services for their long-term care. To find out more information click on the link below.

https://www.dhs.wisconsin.gov/iris/index.htm

Department Of Labor - Office of Disability Employment Policy

Developed by the Office of Disability Employment Policy, "Skills to Pay the Bills: Mastering Soft Skills for Workplace Success" is a curriculum created to help introduce and develop workplace interpersonal and professional skills. The program targets the youth ages 14 - 21 and provides activities focusing on six key skill areas. To learn more about the curriculum click on the link below.

Skills To Pay The Bills: 
https://www.dol.gov/odep/topics/youth/softskills/

Autism Society of Southeast Wisconsin

An organization who strives to be the a leading resource for Autism/Asperger Syndrome PDD in Southeastern Wisconsin. For more information click on the links below.
