GrowingRoots

Our mission is to provide social and employability skills training so that individuals with disabilities can enjoy meaningful, integrated employment allowing greater opportunities for community engagement.

Who We Are

Social skills training groups with a focus on the soft skills needed to acquire meaningful employment and sustain lasting friendships

What We Do

We facilitate social skills instruction in a natural environment with plenty of opportunity for practice, immediate feedback and restructuring. How We Help

According to 2017 Department of Labor statistics 18.7% of persons with disabilities were employed compared to 65.7% of those without disabilities. Even persons with disabilities with post-secondary education are less likely to be employed than persons with post-secondary education without disabilities. There is no reason that persons with disabilities should be underemployed and have their talents under-utilized. We are out to be part of the movement that sees the employment rate of persons with disabilities rise!

What Seperates Us

While there is a great need for services like ours, there are very few places that offer them. We are centrally located for the greater Milwaukee Metro area (in Wauwatosa), and you get two seasoned professionals for the price of one!
